Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Pixupid offer?

We specialize in three main areas: Branding (logos, brand identity, marketing materials), Presentations (professional pitch decks and corporate presentations), and Custom Doodles (personalized artwork for gifts and special occasions).

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple doodles typically take 3-5 business days, branding projects 2-3 weeks, and presentations 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design process?

We start with understanding your goals and message, followed by storyboarding and concept development. You'll receive initial drafts for feedback, and we iterate until you're completely satisfied with the final design.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! We include multiple revision rounds in all our packages to ensure the final product perfectly matches your vision. Quality and client satisfaction are our top priorities.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive high-resolution files in various formats suitable for both print and digital use, including PNG, JPG, PDF, and source files when applicable.

How do I get started?

Simply reach out via our contact page or email us at design@pixupid.com. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project needs.
